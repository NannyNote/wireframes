🏗️ Structure

Stat strip is fixed — sits between the header and the scroll area, always visible. Only the content below it scrolls. Send button and bottom nav are fixed at the bottom. Nothing clips.

🕐 Timeline card

Auto-pulled from today's log entries. Chronological, with category dots and photo thumbnails inline. Gives the nanny a visual recap before writing — also feeds the AI with context.

✦ AI panel

Generate from logs — reads the timeline, writes a warm diary entry. Polish my writing — refines the nanny's own text. Forest green header, warm tone only badge. Always editable before sending.

⌨️ Sticky AI bar

When keyboard opens, the AI panel compresses into a compact sticky bar above the keys. Both Generate and Polish always accessible while typing.

📋 Handover

Gold accent, No AI badge. Quick-fill chips for common items — tap to append. Free text below. The nanny's words sent exactly as written.

🚩 Flag system

All good · Just so you know · Worth discussing · Needs attention. Controls the notification weight. Red sends a push immediately, others are softer.
