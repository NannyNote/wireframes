🕐 Timeline card

Auto-pulled from today's log entries — chronological, with category dots and photo thumbnails inline. Gives the nanny a quick visual recap before writing. Helps the AI generate something specific rather than generic.

✦ AI writing panel

Generate from logs — reads the timeline and writes a warm diary entry. Polish my writing — refines what the nanny has typed. Both modes in one panel with a forest green header. Warm tone only. Always editable before sending.

⌨️ Sticky AI bar

When the keyboard opens, the AI panel compresses into a sticky bar above the keys. Generate and Polish buttons always accessible while typing. ⌄ dismisses the keyboard without losing any work.

📋 Handover card

Gold accent, "No AI" badge. Quick-fill chips for common handover items — tap to add, free text below. The nanny's practical words sent exactly as written.

🚩 Flag system

Four levels: All good · Just so you know · Worth discussing · Needs attention. Controls the notification weight sent to parents. Red sends a push immediately, others are softer.

🔒 Lock on send

Tapping Send writes to Supabase, fires parent notification, and locks narrative, handover and flag immediately. Cannot be changed. Consistent with medication immutability across the app.
