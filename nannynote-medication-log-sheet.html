⚖️ Legal grade design

"Legal record" tag in the sheet header, lavender accent on all borders and inputs, confirmation overlay before saving. The visual language is serious and deliberate from the moment the sheet opens.

🔍 Medication dropdown

Pre-populated with common children's medications. Typing filters in real time. Most medications found in 2–3 keystrokes. Free text "Other" option for anything not in the list.

⏰ Next dose suggestion

App pre-suggests the next dose time based on the medication selected. Calpol → every 4–6 hrs. Nurofen → every 6–8 hrs. Nanny can adjust the suggested time or skip the reminder entirely. Nanny-side notification only — not visible to parents.

🔒 Confirmation overlay

Full-screen — not a dialog. Lists every detail: medication, dose, child, time, reason, given by, next dose. Warning explicitly states it cannot be edited or deleted. "Go back and edit" always available.

📩 Parent notification

Fires automatically on save. No manual trigger needed. Parent receives: "Olivia was given Calpol 5ml at 2:15 pm. Tap to view full record."
