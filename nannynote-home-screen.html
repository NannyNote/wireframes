📋 Plan card

Matches your screenshot — white rounded card (18px radius), 46px icon in a rounded square, bold forest green title (DM Sans 700), light grey subtitle. The only change from your screenshot is "Up next · [event] · [time]" replacing "4 things scheduled".

⏭ Up next

The subtitle line shows the immediately relevant next event. If nothing is scheduled, it shows "Nothing planned · free afternoon". If the plan is expanded, the chevron rotates to ▴. Tapping the card navigates to the full day plan.

← Swipe left

Card slides left 100px. Edit (forest green, 52px) and Delete (red, 48px) are revealed at full card height — they fill the card-row container cleanly. The card-row clips both the card and the actions with matching border-radius. Tap elsewhere to dismiss.

🔒 Locked entries

Medication and Incident entries show a single lavender action (100px) on swipe. LOCKED badge appears on the card face itself. Tapping opens a read-only view. Neither edit nor delete is offered — legal grade, immutable by design.

🌑 Shadows

Cards, plan card and quick log buttons all share the same two-layer shadow system: 0 2px 10px rgba(0,0,0,0.08) for depth, 0 1px 3px rgba(0,0,0,0.05) for a crisp edge. Consistent depth throughout the screen.
