Toddler — manual times

Toddler sheet is simpler — no wake window, yes/no nap question first
Manual time entry instead of live timer — toddler naps are easier to log retrospectively
Dropped nap milestone checkbox — flags the significant moment when a toddler stops napping

⏱️ Timer background persistence

When the nanny starts the timer and closes the sheet or navigates away, the timer keeps running in the background. A small sky blue indicator appears in the home screen header showing "Olivia sleeping · 1h 12m". Tapping it re-opens the sleep sheet with the running timer.

🧒 Age-based sheet version

The sheet shown (baby vs toddler) is determined by the child's age in their profile. Under 18 months: baby sheet with live timer and wake windows. 18 months – 3 years: toddler sheet with manual times and dropped nap flag. Can be overridden in child settings.

⏰ Wake window calculation

The wake window banner is auto-calculated from the last logged wake time and the child's age. 0–3 months: 45–60 min. 3–6 months: 1.5–2 hrs. 6–12 months: 2–2.5 hrs. Shown as a gentle reference — not a notification or alarm. Turns amber if the nap is running unusually long.
